Scholarship Opportunities

🎓 National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Description: A one-stop portal for Government scholarships for students in India.

Eligibility: Students from Class 1 to PhD with family income below ₹8 LPA.

Benefits: Tuition fee waiver, maintenance allowance, and exam fee support.

Deadline: Usually opens in August–November every year.

Official Website
🌍 DAAD Scholarships (Germany)

Description: German Academic Exchange Service offers scholarships for Indian students to pursue Masters/PhD in Germany.

Eligibility: UG degree with strong academics, proof of German/English proficiency.

Benefits: Monthly stipend of ~€850, travel allowance, health insurance.

Deadline: Varies (usually September–October).

Official Website
🇬🇧 Chevening Scholarships (UK)

Description: UK Government scholarships for one-year fully funded Master's programs.

Eligibility: At least 2 years of work experience + good academics.

Benefits: Full tuition fees, monthly stipend, travel costs.

Deadline: Applications usually open in August.

Official Website
🇺🇸 Fulbright-Nehru Masters Fellowships (USA)

Description: Scholarships for Indian students to pursue a Master’s degree in the USA.

Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree + leadership potential + 3 years work experience.

Benefits: Tuition fees, living expenses, accident & sickness coverage, airfare.

Deadline: Opens in January, closes in June.
